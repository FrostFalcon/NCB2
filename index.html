Infinite Black 2





Welcome to Pokemon Infinite Black 2: A rom hack made to add variety, replayability, and explore new ideas. Some key features include massively expanded encounter pools and reworks to many pokemon to make them more unique. It also works great for nuzlockes and other challenge runs that would offer room for new Pokemon to shine.
